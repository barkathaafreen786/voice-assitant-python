LIST OF SUPPORTED COMMANDS 


Say This
	It Should Do This
	"Open notepad"
	Launches Notepad
	"What is the time?"
	Speaks the current time
	"Search AI tools"
	Opens browser with Google search
	"Tell me a joke"
	Says a joke
	"Take note"
	Saves your spoken note to note.txt
	"Show note"
	Reads back saved notes
	"Exit" or "Stop"
	Closes the program
